About Us

We are a two-man development team passionate about pushing the boundaries of Dart and Flutter. Self-taught and driven by innovation, we specialize in building both backend and frontend solutions using Dart and Flutter.

With extensive experience in backend development using Dart packages like dart_frog, and numerous published frontend apps using Flutter, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality, performant, and user-friendly applications.

Our favorite storage solutions include MongoDB for cloud storage and Isar for local storage. However, we are well-versed in various other storage solutions such as SQL, ObjectBox, Shared Preferences, CosmosDB, Firebase Storage, and Firebase Realtime Database.
