Deleting Your Account

To delete your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Log into the App: Open the BeanBreak app on your device and log in using your credentials (email and password).

  2. Go to the Profile Page: Once you’re logged in, navigate to the profile page.

  3. Click the Delete Account Button: On the profile page, look for an option or button labeled “Delete Account”. Click on it to initiate the account deletion process.

  4. Confirm Deletion: After clicking the “Delete Account” button, the app may ask you to confirm your decision. Follow the prompts and confirm that you indeed want to delete your account.

Once you’ve completed these steps, your account will be deleted from BeanBreak. Please note that this action is irreversible, and all your data associated with the account will be permanently removed.

If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team at dynamitetechdeals@gmail.com.

Please note that by deleting your account, you will lose access to all features and data associated with your account.
